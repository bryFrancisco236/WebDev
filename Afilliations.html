Welcome to the Affiliations section! Here, you'll find a comprehensive overview of the esteemed organizations and partners that I collaborate with and are a part of. Each organization represents a significant contribution to my improvement. I have made friends, and formed stronger relationships with my colleagues in these organizations.

Computer Studies College Council

The Computer Studies College Council (CSCC) at the University of Nueva Caceres works to create a supportive and engaging environment within the College of Computer Studies. Guided by the mantra "CCS is FUTURE-READY: Learning w/o Borders", it creates a strong organization of students who aspire to be the future pioneers of our innovative world. It achieves this by organizing events, workshops, and seminars to enhance student participation, facilitating communication between students and faculty, supporting professional development through various programs, encouraging collaboration on projects, and organizing social events to enrich campus life. The CSCC aims to foster both personal and professional growth for CCS students through these initiatives.

Black Box Society

The Black Box Society, a UNC College of Computer Studies-based organization, is an organization that aims to develop the talents and skills of every ACT, IT, CS, and LIS student and mold them to become future-ready individuals in this fast-growing nation.

Social Arts Council

The Social Arts Council is a council/organization for the subject of 21st Century Learning course of the University of Nueva Caceres. The UNC SocArts Council aims to guide and instill the value of the subject, and what it means being a UNCean to the students of UNC. It is composed of leaders who are responsive, proactive, and future-ready.
